                                                              Article I  5
    (aa)     “Extension” means an amendment to a Player Contract
lengthening the term of the Contract for a specified period of years.
    (bb)     “First Round Pick” means a player selected by a Team in the
first round of the Draft.
    (cc)     “Free Agent” means: (i) a Veteran Free Agent; (ii) a Rookie
Free Agent; (iii) a Veteran whose Player Contract has been terminated in
accordance with the NBA waiver procedure; or (iv) a player whose last
Player Contract was a 10-Day Contract and who either completed the
Contract by rendering the playing services called for thereunder or was
released early from such Contract.
    (dd)     “Generally Recognized League Honors” means the following
NBA league honors awarded to players: NBA Most Valuable Player; NBA
Finals Most Valuable Player; NBA Defensive Player of the Year; NBA
Sixth Man Award; NBA Most Improved Player; All-NBA Team (First,
Second, or Third); NBA All-Defensive Team (First or Second); and All-
Star Team Selection.
    (ee)     “Inactive List” means the list of players, maintained by the
NBA, who have signed Player Contracts with a Team and are otherwise
ineligible to participate in a Regular Season game.
    (ff)     “Incentive Compensation” means the component of
Compensation consisting of one (1) or more bonuses described in Article
II, Sections 3(b)(ii) and (iii) and 3(c).
    (gg)     “Likely Bonus” means Incentive Compensation included in a
player’s Salary in accordance with Article VII, Section 3(d).
    (hh)     “Maximum Annual Salary” means the maximum amount of
Salaries and Unlikely Bonuses a player is eligible to receive in the first
Salary Cap Year covered by a Contract or Extension as calculated in
accordance with Article II, Section 7.
    (ii)     “Member” or “Team” means any team that is a member of the
NBA.
    (jj)     “Minimum Annual Salary” means the minimum Salary that
must be included in a Player Contract (other than a Two-Way Contract)
