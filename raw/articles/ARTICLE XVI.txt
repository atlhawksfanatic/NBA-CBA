ARTICLE XVI
MUTUAL RESERVATION OF RIGHTS
Upon the expiration or termination of this Agreement, no person shall
be deemed to have waived, by reason of the entry into or effectuation of
this Agreement, any other collective bargaining agreement, or any Player
Contract, or any of the terms of any of them, or by reason of any practice
or course of dealing, their respective rights under law with respect to any
issue or their ability to advance any legal argument.
